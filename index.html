The Sourcing

San Marzano tomatoes from the slopes of Mount Vesuvius. Fresh mozzarella delivered daily from local artisan farms.

02

The Ferment

Our dough rests for exactly 72 hours. This slow fermentation creates a light, airy crust with a complex, tangy flavor.

03

The Fire

Baked in a hand-built imported Neapolitan oven at exactly 900°F (482°C) for 90 seconds. Charred, crisp, perfect.
